New York Pizzas

New York City ia known as the CAPITAL of the world. It boast almost every culture imaginable, including food! One of the major tourist attraction of New York is Pizza! Yes! what is a visit to New York without tasting its famous neopolitan style pizzas! From thin crust to Sicilian slices there is slice for everyone! Not to mention, it is the most popular city where you can find a slice of pizza for just 1 dollar! Indeed, everyone can afford a slice in this American Dream!

The History of New York Pizzas

New York's history of pizzas start with immigrants coming from all around the world. Particularly from Italy, immigrants came with ideas of bread, cheese and tomatoe sauce. Many immigrants were poor so they wanted to sell their goods, particularly food, any way they could. Pizza was affordable and eassy to produce because of it light weight. It also featured few ingredients. Many laborers and even children could eat slices of pizza without making much of a mess. Soon this street food became popular among New Yorkers and a pizzeria could be found on just about every corner. Now, New York cannot be New York without its pizza!
